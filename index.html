TFOE Shorts

Non-canon material that depicts Siddharth and Tiracha in strange situations from other books or pop culture media and whose sole purpose is to leave you with more questions than answers.

The Other Side (Siddharth, 2028): "I didn't want to do it, but I had no choice. I pulled out the sniper and stood behind the pillar. Just when I was ready to shoot, I was shot in the leg. I screamed, and the man approached me to interrogate me. I was impersonating, so I couldn't give him the details. In a desperate and probably hopeless attempt, I pulled out the frag I had bought at Botha's Firearms. I expected him to shoot again before I pulled the pin off, but he didn't. He ran, but was caught in the blast radius. I felt really guilty about it, but Tiracha needed this. My invincibility cheat kept me unharmed, but I didn't want to look suspicious or be hunted down, so I spawned a corpse of myself, called the police using the man's phone (whose name was apparently Liu Bingwen) and escaped the scene before the cops could arrive."
